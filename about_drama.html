What are Fans Talking About Most

Based on the reviews crawled from mydramalist, I use NLTK to analyse the text and find out the words that fans discussed most about this drama based on the words frequency in reviews.

I also use word cloud to generate a word cloud graph to give people a straitforward impression of the frequent keywords.

  • More

After filtered the no meaningful words, I found that the drama characters and casts are cared most by the fans, which also matches the average rating result that 'cast' category gets higher mark than 'story' and 'rewatch' value.

It is noticable that besides the leading roles - Ha Ni(91) and Seung Jo(55), even supporting roles like Seung Jo mother(6) and Ha Ni friend(3) are discussed in reviews.
