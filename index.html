About Playful Kiss (2010)

Playful Kiss (Hangul: 장난스런 키스; RR: Jangnanseureon Kiseu; also known as Mischievous Kiss or Naughty Kiss) is a 2010 South Korean romantic-comedy television series. It aired on MBC from September 1 to October 21, 2010 on Wednesdays and Thursdays at 21:55 for 16 episodes.

It is based on the Japanese manga 'Itazura Na Kiss' written by Tada Kaoru. The Korean series is the third television adaptation of the manga.

Through 'Playful Kiss' received low ratings in South Korea in the five to seven percent range, it was sold to 12 countries in Asia for approximately US$3,400,000 and developed a strong cult following, having been streamed 70 million times on Viki and earning US$400,000 in ad revenue through online streaming.

Culture Acceptance

'Playful Kiss(2010)' is based on the popular Japanese manga 'Itazura Na Kiss', so it is no wonder that either manga or TV shows is fancinated by young people in Asia.

Due to this reason, I searched the ratings of this drama from other sites, including douban(the most popular movie rating site in China) and imdb(a widely well-known movie rating site in global), to compare the rating differences between sites and find out the culture influences of drama.
